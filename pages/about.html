ABOUTBALI-PHONE

It was 1997 when Faisal Al Bannai decided that there was a better way to trade and distribute mobile devices and accessories. Axiom telecom was born - and now it’s all grown up. Today, under the leadership of Fahad Al Bannai, our CEO, we’re the largest distributor of mobile technology products in the region. We supply to thousands of retailers, both independent and big consumer brands alike. Our own brand Axiom Retail and Online Stores are famous for their unrivalled levels of service, our unique range of Handsets, accessories and related services and the high tech shopping experience they provide. We’re the major authorized retailer and distributor of some of the world’s most recognizable brands in the mobile technology world - but we don’t stop there! We take our experience and know-how and develop our own products and services to best meet the needs of our customers in the region. We know better than anyone the quality that our customers insist upon. We also deal with various telecom operator partners in each of the countries we operate in, ensuring we can offer our customers a complete mobile device solution. The world has changed a lot since 1997, and so have we! Our goal has always been to revolutionize our industry so we never stop moving, we never stop transforming, every day! We have bold plans for our future; we hope you will be a part of them.
